About TechVerse

TechVerse is the annual technology festival organized by the Department of Computer Science. The event brings together students, developers and innovators to participate in exciting competitions, workshops and technical sessions. Whether you enjoy programming, robotics, artificial intelligence or web development, TechVerse provides an opportunity to learn, compete and network with experts.
